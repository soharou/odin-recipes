Pancakes

Description

As convenient as a store-bought mix can be, every home cook needs a good buttermilk pancake recipe in their repertoire.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • 3 Tbsp of white sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • 3 eggs
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt) in a large bowl.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients (buttermilk, milk, eggs, melted butter) in a separate bowl. Do not combine the dry and wet ingredients until before you cook the pancakes.
  3. Make the pancakes. Combine all ingredients and mix with a wooden spoon or fork until just-blended.
  4. Scoop the batter onto a hot, oiled griddle and cook until pancake is bubbly on the top. Flip and cook until both sides are brown.
